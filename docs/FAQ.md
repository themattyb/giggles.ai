## About giggles.ai

### What is giggles.ai?

giggles.ai is a community-driven platform focused on making AI learning fun, accessible, and engaging. We believe that humor and play are essential components of effective learning, especially when it comes to complex topics like artificial intelligence.

### What makes giggles.ai different from other AI learning platforms?

- **Community-First**: Built by and for the community, not by a corporation
- **Fun-Focused**: We prioritize making learning enjoyable and engaging
- **Open Source**: Everything is transparent and community-owned
- **Beginner-Friendly**: Designed to welcome people of all skill levels
- **Experimental**: We celebrate "failures" and quirky results as learning opportunities

### Is this a serious educational platform?

Absolutely! While we use humor and play as learning tools, we're committed to providing high-quality, accurate educational content about artificial intelligence. Fun doesn't mean frivolous â€“ it means effective.

### What does "where AI meets joy" mean?

It's our core philosophy that learning about AI should be joyful, not intimidating. We want to break down the barriers that make AI seem scary or inaccessible to many people.

### I'm completely new to AI. Can I still participate?

Yes! giggles.ai is designed with beginners in mind. You don't need any prior AI experience to join our community or start learning.

## Contributing

### I found a bug. How do I report it?

1. Check if the bug has already been reported in our [Issues](https://github.com/your-username/giggles.ai/issues)
2. If not, create a new issue with:
   - Clear description of the problem
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Expected vs. actual behavior
   - Screenshots if relevant

### How do I suggest a new feature?

1. Check our [Discussions](https://github.com/your-username/giggles.ai/discussions) to see if it's been discussed
2. Open a new discussion or issue with:
   - Clear description of the feature
   - Why it would be valuable
   - How it fits with our mission

### I want to write content. What topics are needed?

We're always looking for:
- Beginner-friendly AI explanations
- Fun, interactive tutorials
- Real-world AI applications
- Ethical AI discussions
- Historical perspectives on AI development
- Career guidance in AI

### Can I contribute if I don't know how to code?

Absolutely! Non-code contributions are incredibly valuable:
- Writing documentation and tutorials
- Testing and providing feedback
- Community moderation and support
- Design and visual content
- Translation and localization
- Social media and outreach

### How long does it take to get pull requests reviewed?

We aim to review pull requests within 3-5 business days. Complex PRs might take longer, but we'll communicate if we need more time.

## Technical Questions

### What technologies does giggles.ai use?

Currently:
- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, vanilla JavaScript
- **Hosting**: Static hosting (GitHub Pages, Netlify, or similar)
- **Version Control**: Git/GitHub

Future plans include:
- JavaScript frameworks for interactive features
- TensorFlow.js for client-side AI demos
- Backend services for community features

### Why start with such simple technology?

We believe in starting simple and growing organically. This approach:
- Makes it easy for beginners to contribute
- Keeps the initial learning curve low
- Allows us to focus on content and community first
- Provides a solid foundation for future complexity

### Will you add a backend/database eventually?

Yes! As outlined in our [Roadmap](ROADMAP.md), we plan to add backend services for user accounts, community features, and more advanced functionality.

### Can I use giggles.ai content in my own projects?

Yes! Our content is open source under our [License](../LICENSE). Please check the specific license terms and provide appropriate attribution.

### How do you ensure the AI information is accurate?

- Community review process for all educational content
- Expert contributors who verify technical accuracy
- Regular content audits and updates
- Clear sourcing and references for all claims

## Community

### What are your community guidelines?

Our community guidelines are based on:
- **Be Kind**: Treat everyone with respect and empathy
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome people of all backgrounds and skill levels
- **Be Constructive**: Focus on helpful, actionable feedback
- **Be Playful**: Don't forget to have fun!

See our full [Code of Conduct](CONTRIBUTING.md#code-of-conduct) for details.

### How do I get help if I'm stuck?

1. **GitHub Discussions**: Ask questions and get community help
2. **Discord**: Real-time community chat (coming soon)
3. **Documentation**: Check our comprehensive guides
4. **Issues**: For bug reports and technical problems

### Can I organize a local giggles.ai meetup?

We'd love that! Reach out to us at [hello@giggles.ai](mailto:hello@giggles.ai) and we'll help you get started.

Yes! We recognize contributors through:
- Contributor spotlights in our README
- Monthly community highlights
- Special contributor badges (coming soon)

## Legal & Licensing

### What license does giggles.ai use?

The project uses an open source license (see [LICENSE](../LICENSE) file). This allows you to use, modify, and distribute the code freely.

### Can I use giggles.ai for commercial purposes?

Please check our specific license terms. Generally, open source licenses allow commercial use, but attribution requirements may apply.

### Who owns the content I contribute?

When you contribute to giggles.ai, you retain copyright to your contributions, but you grant the project permission to use them under our open source license.

### How do you handle privacy and data?

Currently, as a static website, we collect minimal data. As we grow:
- We'll always be transparent about data collection
- We'll prioritize user privacy and control
- We'll follow GDPR and other applicable privacy laws
- We'll never sell user data

### What about AI ethics and bias?

AI ethics is central to our mission:
- We discuss bias and fairness in AI systems
- We promote diverse perspectives in our community
- We encourage critical thinking about AI's impact
- We support responsible AI development practices
